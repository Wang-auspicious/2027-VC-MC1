## Segment 5 — Governance carryover

On screen: The uncertainty-aware IGC radar and evidence ranges.

Voiceover: The three answers converge on Inter-Agent Governance Carryover: whether a constraint closes the relevant route, transfers across public surfaces, survives over time, preserves provenance, and retains legitimate utility. June fifth shows the consequence of lost carryover. Each dimension remains linked to source messages and explicitly marks partial or unobserved evidence.
